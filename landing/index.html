VIBE HUB

Build the software you want for yourself and your friends with vibe coding.

With AI tools such as Claude Code, making apps is easier, faster, and (arguably) more fun than ever. By offloading the engineering work to AI, we can focus on the higher-level work of considering what to build, for whom, and why. Instead of relying on expensive apps released by large tech companies, build what you need yourself!

Vibe Hub is a PC Desktop app that lets you create, manage, overlook, and iterate on your vibe coding projects. It integrates with Claude Code. It makes vibe coding more fun, and helps you make better apps.

I hope you will use it to bring something meaningful into this world!
